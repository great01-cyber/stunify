How It Works

Getting started is simple

1

Sign Up with Student Email

Register using your university email (.ac.uk). Verified students unlock full access to donate, request, and receive items. Non-students can still donate to support the community.

2

Browse or List Items

Explore items shared by other students‚Äîfrom furniture to textbooks, kitchenware to electronics. Have something to share? Create a listing with photos and descriptions in minutes.

3

Connect & Arrange

Use in-app messaging to coordinate pickup details. Meet safely on campus in public locations during daylight hours. Build community while reducing waste.

4

Share the Love

Complete the exchange and help build a sustainable student community. Rate your experience and continue the cycle of sharing.
